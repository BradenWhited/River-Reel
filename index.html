In Post - 2BR02B

Based on a short story of the same name penned by the late Kurt Vonnegut Jr, 2BR02B (Pronounced “To Be Or Not To Be”) is the story of Edward Wheling, a man faced with an impossible decision in a dystopic future. 2B offers a great deal of insight into the potential moral, ethical, and emotional issues that the future may hold for mankind due to the rapid advancement of science and medicines.

Recently Released - The Curiosity Shoppe

Shot for The 100 Hour Film Race, “The Curiosity Shoppe” weaves a tangled web of intrigue. Follow the story of Anthony, as he discovers the dark secret behind a seemingly normal porcelain doll. When Anthony starts to see visions, he sets out to save the individual in these new nightmares of his.
